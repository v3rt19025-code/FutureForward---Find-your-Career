Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FutureForward, our career assessment, and how we help students plan their future.

Is FutureForward free to use?

Yes! FutureForward is completely free for all students. Our mission is to make career planning and education funding accessible to everyone, regardless of background or location.

How accurate is the career matching?

Our career recommendations are based on real job market data and proven assessment methods. While no tool can predict your future with 100% certainty, FutureForward provides personalized insights based on your interests, skills, and preferences to help guide your decision-making.

What languages does FutureForward support?

FutureForward currently supports English, Russian, and Kazakh. We're working on expanding to more languages to serve diverse communities worldwide.

How long does the assessment take?

The full assessment takes about 10 minutes to complete. It's divided into three simple steps: career interests, education planning, and personal information.

Do I need to provide personal information?

Most of the assessment is optional. We only require basic information to provide personalized recommendations. Any personal data you share is used solely for generating your results and is not stored or shared with third parties.

Can I retake the assessment?

Absolutely! You can retake the assessment as many times as you'd like. This is helpful if your interests change or you want to explore different scenarios.

What if I'm not sure about my interests?

That's completely normal! FutureForward is designed to help you explore and discover your interests. If you're unsure, we recommend taking the assessment anyway - it can help clarify your thoughts and provide new perspectives.

How do you calculate funding estimates?

Our funding calculations are based on average costs for different education paths and typical scholarship/grant availability. These are estimates to help you plan - we recommend consulting with financial aid offices for personalized advice.

Can I save or download my results?

Yes! After completing the assessment, you can download your results as a Word document for your records. This includes your career recommendations, funding plan, and CV preview.

Who can use FutureForward?

FutureForward is designed for students at any stage - from high school students exploring careers to college applicants planning their education. Anyone interested in making smarter decisions about their future can benefit.
