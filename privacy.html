Privacy Policy

Data Usage & Protection

At FutureForward, we are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we use your information.

How We Use Your Information

We collect information about your interests, goals, skills, and educational background solely for the purpose of:

  • Evaluating your profile and preferences
  • Providing personalized career recommendations
  • Calculating funding options tailored to your situation
  • Building a professional CV that showcases your strengths

What We Don't Do

We do NOT use your information for:

  • Marketing or advertising purposes
  • Selling or sharing data with third parties
  • Tracking or profiling beyond the scope of your assessment
  • Any purpose outside of providing you with personalized results

Your Data is Safe

Your information is encrypted, secure, and only used to benefit you. We respect your privacy and maintain the highest standards of data protection.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us.
